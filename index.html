I have recently graduated from MIT, and will be beginning in late August this year as a graduate student in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Pennsylvania. My research interests lie in the realm of quantum gravity, including the mathematical structure of string theory and quantum field theories, the black hole information problem, applications of holography to statistical physics and strongly correlated systems, and more. On this website, you can find more information about my publications, current research projects, teaching activites, resources for learning physics, and personal interests.
